About Lets Go:

Lets go allows users to find the most popularly visited and highly rated places in within the city they currently find themselves in. If another place sparks a users interest and is not currently available to view, users may add this place. More information including ratings, reviews and directions are readily available for users.

Added features include planning future trips and seeing additional places close to already existing ones.
